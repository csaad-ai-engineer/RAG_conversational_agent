# Appointment Booking FAQ

## How do patients book an appointment?
Patients search for a practitioner by specialty or location, select an available time slot from the
practitioner's calendar, and confirm the booking. A confirmation email and SMS reminder are sent
automatically 24 hours before the appointment.

## Can a patient reschedule an appointment?
Yes. Patients can reschedule up to 2 hours before the appointment start time directly from their
account. Rescheduling within 2 hours requires contacting the practitioner's office directly.

## What happens if a patient cancels late?
Cancellations made less than 1 hour before the appointment may be marked as a late cancellation.
Practitioners can configure whether late cancellations incur a fee, depending on their office policy.

## Why is a time slot no longer available after I selected it?
Slots are held for 5 minutes during checkout. If payment or confirmation is not completed within
that window, the slot is released back into the practitioner's calendar for other patients.
