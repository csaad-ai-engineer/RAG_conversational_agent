# Billing FAQ

## How are consultation fees charged?
For most practitioners, payment is collected at the end of the consultation via the office's usual
method (card, cash, or health insurance card for direct billing). Some practitioners enable online
prepayment for teleconsultations.

## What if a patient was charged twice for the same appointment?
Duplicate charges are automatically flagged by the payment reconciliation system within 24 hours and
refunded to the original payment method. Patients can also report duplicate charges directly through
support for manual review.

## How do practitioners receive payments for online prepayments?
Online prepayments are settled to the practitioner's registered bank account on a rolling basis,
typically within 3 to 5 business days after the appointment is marked as completed.

## What is a no-show fee?
A no-show fee may be charged when a patient does not attend a booked appointment without cancelling.
Whether a no-show fee applies, and its amount, is configured individually by each practitioner.
