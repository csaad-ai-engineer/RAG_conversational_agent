# GDPR and Health Data Privacy FAQ

## How is patient health data protected?
All health-related data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and is hosted in certified health-data
facilities (HDS-certified in France, or equivalent regional certification). Access is restricted to
authorized staff on a need-to-know basis.

## Can a patient request deletion of their data?
Yes. Under GDPR, patients can request full deletion of their personal data by submitting a request
through account settings or contacting the data protection officer. Deletion requests are processed
within 30 days, except for data legally required to be retained (e.g., billing records).

## Who can access a patient's appointment history?
Only the patient and the practitioners they have booked appointments with can access appointment
history. Internal support staff can only access data with explicit patient consent for
troubleshooting purposes, and all such access is logged.

## Is patient data shared with third parties?
Patient data is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Limited technical data
may be shared with infrastructure subprocessors strictly to operate the service, under data
processing agreements compliant with GDPR.
